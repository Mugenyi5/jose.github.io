Mbere yo gukina umukino wa gicuti n’Ikipe y’Igihugu ya Suède kuri uyu wa Mbere, abagize Ikipe ya Espagne bose babanje gupimwa icyorezo cya Covid-19. Ibisubizo byerekanye ko Busquets usanzwe ukinira FC Barcelona arwaye iki cyorezo ndetse ahita ashyirwa mu kato. Ni mu gihe kandi habura iminsi irindwi ngo irushanwa rya Euro 2020, ritangire, amakipe y’ibihugu yabonye itike ubu akomeje imyiteguro irimo no gukina imikino ya gicuti itandukanye. Nubwo Sergio Busquets yasanzwemo icyorezo cya Covid-19, bagenzi be bo bakomeje imyitozo ndetse kuri uyu wa Mbere barakina umukino wa gicuti na Suède, hanyuma ku wa Kabiri bazahure na Lithuania. Uyu mugabo w’imyaka 32 y’amavuko, bisobanuye ko mu gihe yakomeza gupimwa agasangwa Covid-19, atakina umukino w’umunsi wa mbere kuko habura iminsi irindwi gusa ngo iri rushanwa ritangire.
