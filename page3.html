Bron James w’imyaka 36 y’amavuko, yahaye iyi nimero ye Anthony Davis nk’impano yamugeneye, nyuma y’uko uyu musore yaje muri Los Angeles Lakers mu 2019. LeBron James yahise avuga agomba gufata nimero 6 n’ubundi yigeze kwambara agikinira ikipe ya Miami Heat kuva mu 2011-2013. Kuva mu mwaka utaha w’imikino, LeBron James azaba yambara nimero 6, ndetse zanatangiye kugurishwa ku bwinshi muri Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika. LeBron James ni umugabo w‘izina rinini mu mukino wa Basketball muri Leta Zunze Ubumwe za Amerika, kuko amaze kwegukana ibikombe bya shampiyona ya NBA, bigera kuri bine (2012, 2013, 2016 na 2020). Yagiye yegukana ibihembo byo ku giti cye byinshi, birimo bine yabaye MVP (2012, 2013, 2016 na 2020) yabaye umwiza mu bakinnyi b‘intoranywa inshuro eshatu (NBA All-Star) mu 2006, 2008 na 2018. Ni umugabo wagiye yiharira ibihembo byinshi, yaba ibyo yagiye atwarana n’amakipe yakinnyemo n’ibyo ku giti cye. James yakiniye Cleveland Cavaliers (2003-2010), Miami Heat (2010-2014), asubira muri Cleveland Cavaliers (2014-2018), mu 2018 ahava ajya muri Los Angeles Lakers akiri kugeza ubu. Anthony Davis kuva mu 2012 kugeza ubu, nimero 23 ni yo yakunze kwambara kuva muri New Orleans Pelicans (2012-2019).
